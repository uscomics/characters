Make Lithophane.atn

This Action will convert a photo or image into a lithophane complete with 3D scene.
Note: Requires a photo or image layer in your current Photoshop document.

Install the Action
A) Drag-and-drop the Make Lithophane.atn on top of your Photoshop app icon.
OR
B) Drag-and-drop the Make Lithophane.atn into the Photoshop folder > Presets > Actions and restart Photoshop.
